How to use SyncSense

This website helps injured individuals and athletes correct their movements to prevent stress and injuries.

There are two ways to use SyncSense:

  1. Using preset movements
    SyncSense comes loaded with sample movements you can select and practice.
  2. Scanning a video (not 100% accurate!)
    Upload a clear video of someone performing a movement (e.g., badminton smash). Our AI will detect and store the movement.

After choosing a movement, upload a video of you performing the action. SyncSense will compare your movement to the reference and show you what's correct and what needs improvement. This can be done repeatedly until you are able to perform the action successfully.
